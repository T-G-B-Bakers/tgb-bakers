About T.G.B

Welcome to T.G.B Bakers, where every bite is a taste of pure joy! Established in 2019, we are a passionate team dedicated to crafting delightful treats that bring happiness with every bite. Located in the heart of Embakasi, Utawala, our bakery specializes in wholesaling a delectable array of baked goods, including our signature star cookies, heart biscuits, mini cookies, and heart cookies. Each one lovingly prepared to perfection, ensuring that every moment savored is an experience to cherish.
